What we do

  • AI Adoption Training for non-technical teams. Workshops and programmes that change how people work, not just what tools they open.
  • AI Adoption Score. Free 2-minute diagnostic that scores readiness across literacy, governance, workflow, tools and culture.
  • AI Adoption Call. Free 30-minute discovery session. We score maturity and send a 1-page roadmap within 24 hours.
  • EU AI Act Article 4 compliance. AI literacy training that meets the 2 February 2026 obligation for staff dealing with AI systems.

  • Events. Talks, workshops, hackathons and roundtables on responsible AI adoption.
